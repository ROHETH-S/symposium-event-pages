Alien Choices - A DSA-QUIZ

Description:
The participants will have to attend an MCQ for the 1st round where they will be given questions based on DSA.
Those who qualify for the second round have to take up DSA based coding round of varying difficulty levels.
Participants with the highest scores at the end of the event will be announced as winners.

One liner: We quiz therefore we are.

Event Type: Individual event

Rules:
1. Team-size: minimum 1 member.
2. Over writing the answers should be avoided.
3. Mobiles not allowed.
4. The participants are required to have basic knowledge in programming.
5. The event consists of multiple levels. Each level may further consist of subtasks.
6. All the programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python, etc are allowed.
7. Participants must have a HackerRank account before the commencement of the event.
8. Your score will increase as you progress through the tasks.
9. The faster you complete the level, the higher you will be on the leaderboard.
10. Discussion among participants is not encouraged.
