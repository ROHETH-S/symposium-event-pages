Haijo - A Star Event

Description:
The event consists of 3 rounds of which 1 team member will have to be removed by the team leader after each round.
The description of the round will be disclosed on spot so beware of who you remove from the team.
The best performer will be announced as winner.

One liner: Adventure yet to begin

Event Type: Group event

Rules:
1. Team size: minimum 3 members.
2. There will be 3 rounds, at the end of each round you will be asked to remove one member of your team.
3. The type of rounds will be revealed on-spot.
4. Switching of the removed member after the start of the round leads to disqualification of the entire team.
5. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
6. Mobiles are strictly not allowed.
7. Discussion among participants is not encouraged.
8. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
